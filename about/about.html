Andy Caress is a graphic designer studying at Virginia Commonwealth University. He currently works at The Commonwealth Times, an award-winning independent student press at VCU.

As an adopted Asian-American he has always felt like an in-betweener. Not Asian not American, but distinctly Asian-American. He uses graphic design as an extension of his identity, not only for self-expression but also to understand his Asian ethnicity.
